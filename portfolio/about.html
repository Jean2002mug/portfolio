Biography

I’m Jean Michel Mugabe, a Computer Science graduate from Mount Allison University with a minor in Astronomy. My academic and professional focus centers around developing scalable software systems and applying data-driven techniques to solve complex problems.

With strong proficiency in Python, Java, and C, I’ve worked on applications ranging from gravitational physics simulations to sight-reading aids for musicians. My core strengths include algorithm design, data analysis, and software architecture. I’m especially interested in systems optimization, AI model integration, and human-computer interaction.

I’ve collaborated in multi-disciplinary teams, contributed to open-source software, and independently developed full-stack tools and machine learning pipelines. I am now looking for opportunities where I can drive innovation, refine system workflows, and contribute to technically impactful projects.
